# STRIX Trademark and Project Identity Policy

This repository is licensed under Apache-2.0. That license permits use,
copying, modification, and distribution of the covered code, subject to the
license terms. It does not grant rights to the STRIX name, marks, project
identity, or official release channel.

## Official Project

The official public upstream is:

```text
https://github.com/RMANOV/strix
```

Forks must not imply that they are the official STRIX project, an official
release, or an endorsed distribution unless RMANOV has explicitly authorized
that use in writing.

## Allowed Uses

You may use the STRIX name to:

- refer to the original upstream project;
- state that your work is based on or compatible with STRIX;
- preserve required license and attribution notices.

## Restricted Uses

You may not use the STRIX name or confusingly similar branding to:

- present a fork as the official project;
- market a modified distribution as an official STRIX release;
- imply endorsement, certification, partnership, or operational support;
- sell services under the STRIX name without explicit written permission.

## Fork Naming

Forks should use a distinct name and describe their relationship plainly, for
example:

```text
ExampleSwarm, based on STRIX
```

Do not use names such as:

```text
STRIX Enterprise
Official STRIX Pro
STRIX Systems
STRIX NATO Edition
```

unless those uses are explicitly authorized.
